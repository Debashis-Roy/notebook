Welcome to NoteBook


We believe that education should be much more open. Anybody and everybody can learn from anybody
and everybody. Age, gender, social status or class should not be a barrier here.
We also believe that education just doesn’t mean books or lectures. We can learn from anything that is
good. Pictures, videos, arts and even talks can be the source of education.
So join us!
Be a part of our Alapon education system.

Photographs / আলোকচিত্রসমূহ

Everyone has a way to see the world around him. Share your captured pictures in NoteBook to let others know what you see through your brain and eye frame.

Share the pictures taken by any kind of camera.
